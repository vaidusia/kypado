SCOPE

This Privacy Policy applies to KYPADO customers and KYPADO.com visitors, unless a distinct privacy policy is disclosed. KYPADO.com is the online service of KYPADO (Collectively “we,” or “us”). In this statement you will find KYPADO information practices regarding the personal information you provide to us by electronic means. That is to say, how personal information collected online is stored and processed by KYPADO. Please read it carefully, as, by using this website, you acknowledge that you have read and accept our privacy processes.

WHAT PERSONAL INFORMATION IS COLLECTED

KYPADO may collect information provided by you when you use our online service kypado.com, and when you correspond with us by email and social media.
In particular, the personal information or personal data (expression used by the Swedish Personal Data Act) that KYPADO may collect and process are:
  • Name
  • Email address
  • Content of the electronic correspondence between you and KYPADO
Under the Swedish Personal Data Act “personal data” refers to any type of information that directly or indirectly identifies a living natural person.

HOW PERSONAL INFORMATION IS COLLECTED

The personal information detailed above may be collected:
  • Through the contact form when you use our online service kypado.com
  • Through email when you communicate to us by this mean of communication

HOW PERSONAL INFORMATION IS USED

We collect and process your information fairly and lawfully. This information is collected for specific, explicit, and legitimate purposes and not further processed in a way incompatible with such purposes.
We use the information we collect or receive from you to carry out the services you request from KYPADO, such as the preparation, administration, and performance of legal documents requested by you. In addition, we may also use such information for our research activities which include the improvement of our services and the design of future services for you.
We keep personal information about you for the time necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Statement, unless a longer retention period is required by applicable law. Archiving of certain financial information is done pursuant to the applicable accounting legislation, in our case the Swedish Bookkeeping Act (1999:1078), the Annual Accounts Act (1995:1554).

DISCLOSURES

As a general rule, KYPADO will not sell, trade, or disclose to third parties personal information about you without your consent.
There are specific circumstances under which KYPADO is compelled to make an exception to this general rule. This includes the following limited circumstances:
  • To comply with the law, KYPADO will have to disclose information about you to entities, including law enforcement, as required by law or in legal proceedings
  • To protect our rights, safety, or property
  • To service providers and other entities helping us to fulfill the service
We reassure you that we only deal with service providers that have subscribed and adhere to the U.S.-EU/Switzerland Safe Harbor program (“Safe Harbor Program”). More information about the Safe Harbor Program can be found at http://www.export.gov/safeharbor/

SECURITY

KYPADO implements appropriate physical, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. We strive to protect personal information and to keep personal information accurate and up-to-date as necessary. Unfortunately, no security system or system of transmitting data over the Internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

ANALYTICS

KYPADO uses Google Analytics to measure the number of visitors to our website and to analyze user browsing history. KYPADO does not combine the information collected through the use of Google Analytics with your personal information.
Google Analytics uses cookies. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on their servers in the United States. Google has an ability to use and share this information and it may also be used for targeted advertising into the websites. You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser. As stated above, if we do so, the combined information will be treated by KYPADO as personal information in accordance with this Statement.
By using this website, you consent to the processing of data about you by Google in the manner and for the purposes set out above.
